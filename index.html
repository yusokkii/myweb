About me

I'm a Spanish artisan, designer and architect based in Italy. My work is based on the application of craftsmanship in objects and spaces design with a contemporary approach. Over the past 6 years, I have been working in the field of design, providing my clients with the best solutions to create spaces that meet their needs and lifestyle.
